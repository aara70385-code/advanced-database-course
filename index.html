Our signature Half Million Latte is crafted with precision and passion. It starts with our house blend espresso a perfectly balanced combination of Brazilian and Colombian beans, roasted to highlight caramel and chocolate notes.

The espresso is extracted as a double ristretto to concentrate the flavor, then combined with our premium milk that's steamed to a silky microfoam texture. The result is a naturally sweet, velvety smooth latte with a rich mouthfeel and perfect balance between coffee strength and milk sweetness.

Each latte is finished with our signature latte art, making it as beautiful as it is delicious.

Ingredients: Espresso (100% Arabica coffee beans), milk

Contains: Dairy (when made with regular milk)

Espresso blend: 70% Brazil, 30% Colombia

Allergen information: Our products are prepared in an environment that handles nuts, wheat, and other allergens. Please inform our baristas of any allergies when ordering in store.

Regular size with whole milk:

  • Calories: 120 kcal
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Sugar: 8g (naturally occurring in milk)
  • Caffeine: 136mg

Values may vary slightly between batches. Alternative milk options will alter nutritional content.
